Flaming Dr. Pepper Recipe

After the kids are off to bed, you can sit back and enjoy this hot and smooth beverage that tastes just like the legendary soda its named after!!!!

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 cup of beer
  • 1 fluid oz amaretto liqueur
  • 1/4 fluid oz 151 proof rum

Steps

  1. Fill a pint glass half full with beer.
  2. Pour amaretto into a standard shot glass; pour rum on top so it floats.
  3. Light the shot omn fire; carefully drop the lit shot into the half-full glass of beer and drink.
